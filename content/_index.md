  The West Antarctic Ice Sheet (WAIS) Workshop is a  multidisciplinary conference focused on the fate of marine-based ice-sheet sectors. Since its inception in 1992, this long-running workshop has shaped and fostered research on the past and present state of Earth's marine-based sectors (including WAIS), the processes that influence its ongoing change, and the present and future vulnerability of marine-based ice-sheet sectors within a changing Earth system.
