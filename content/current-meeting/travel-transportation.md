## Travel to Northern Virginia 4-H

We recommend you fly into Washington Dulles International Airport, and either carpool or use an airport shuttle service:

Washington Dulles International Airport is an easy 1 hour by car (Google Maps directions [here](https://maps.app.goo.gl/VvznCtXMWYqrryMG7)), depending on traffic and timing the ferry route (if one chooses to take the ferry). Airport shuttle services can be arranged for transportation, but we of course strongly suggest carpooling and, as in years past, we have an electronic ride-sharing board for those who wish to carpool to and from the airport, which you can find [here](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1FpcN7_MKlqs_yMpUe03d1gSovlkj874mbNrzdC87_Bs/edit?usp=sharing). Please carpool!
